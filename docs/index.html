The Daily Loop

A simple 5-step process to keep you on track every single day.

01

Check (Awareness)

Ask yourself: How is my body? How is my mind? What is my energy level? (30-60 seconds)

02

Choose (Intent)

Pick ONE focus area to improve today: Health, Focus, Calm, Money, or Productivity.

03

Act (Adaptive Action)

Execute a Micro, Mini, or Macro action based on your current state.

04

Align (Connection)

Ensure your action connects to your long-term goals. This builds consistency.

05

Adapt (Reflection)

End of day check: What worked? What needs adjustment? (1 minute)
